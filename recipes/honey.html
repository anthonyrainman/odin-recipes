Honey Lavender Latte

The Honey Lavender Latte is a fragrant and soothing coffee drink that combines the floral notes of lavender with the natural sweetness of honey. This latte is perfect for those looking for a relaxing and aromatic twist on their usual coffee routine, offering a delicate balance of flavors that's both comforting and refreshing.
  • 1 cup milk (any kind)
  • 1 shot of espresso (or 1/2 cup strong brewed coffee)
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried culinary lavender (or 1 tablespoon lavender syrup)
  • Optional: Whipped cream and lavender sprigs for garnish
  1. In a small saucepan, combine the milk, honey, and dried lavender (if using). Heat over medium-low heat, stirring occasionally, until the milk is warm and fragrant. Be careful not to let it boil.
  2. Remove the saucepan from heat and let the lavender steep for about 5 minutes. If using dried lavender, strain the milk to remove the lavender buds.
  3. Brew your espresso or strong coffee and pour it into a mug.
  4. Froth the lavender-infused milk using a frother or whisk, then pour it over the espresso.
  5. If using lavender syrup instead of dried lavender, simply stir it into the warm milk before frothing.
  6. Optional: Top with whipped cream and garnish with a sprinkle of lavender or a lavender sprig.
  7. Stir gently and enjoy the calming, aromatic flavors.
